Important
Check-list for users

Always upload a good quality, frontal face photo of both missing persons and found dead bodies. If the dead is decomposed or mutilated because of injuries, try to get a clear photograph after the post-mortem, requesting the Autopsy Surgeon to stitch the face as naturally as possible. The features of the face are essential for automatic identification.

Always record the height of both missing persons and found dead bodies accurately. The dead body's length should be measured precisely after the post-mortem. If the height of a missing person is known exactly because of some ID card which holds that information, please note it. Otherwise, make enquiries with 3/4 close acquaintances to get a best estimate of height (within 5-10 cm).

The app has features to search by location. for this, please record the lat long of the dead body, and, in case of missing persons, ascertain the location where he/she was last seen, and note the lat long.

Enter as much detail about the person as possible in the `Description` column. the app has good search capabilities. Things like wearing apparel, identification marks and habits will be enterd here in one paragraph.
